About our Custom Programs

No two companies are alike nor are any two industries. So, why would you use a learning solution that is cookie-cutter? USC Marshall Executive Education’s custom learning solutions are unique to your company, your industry and your challenges. That means that we get to know your industry and your company, identifying your needs and goals, understanding the culture of your business and people to develop long-term and sustainable solutions to your most complex challenges.

With the resources and faculty of the USC Marshall School of Business, your custom learning program will be delivered by faculty with practical and clinical experience – offering a unique hands-on perspective that includes up-to-minute knowledge of best practices and relevant models. With our comprehensive resources, USC Marshall Executive Education can address nearly any learning topics and most every business concern from marketing to leadership and from strategy to management development.

Our goal in our custom practice is similar to that of our open enrollment classes – deliver solutions that can be put to use on the job immediately, show demonstrable results and deliver a return on investment that will show up on your balance sheet.
