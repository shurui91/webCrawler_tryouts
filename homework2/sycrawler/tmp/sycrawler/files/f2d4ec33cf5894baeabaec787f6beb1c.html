Marshall Spotlight

Iván Gutiérrez
Meet me on LinkedIn
Ten years ago Ivan Gutierrez was a plant manager for Avon in Mexico. He didn’t see a possibility for growth with them, and so sold his apartment and car and invested everything he had in attending the IBEAR program. He explains: “I had a family back home to support, so I had to be very focused. It was a risk, but it paid off.”
