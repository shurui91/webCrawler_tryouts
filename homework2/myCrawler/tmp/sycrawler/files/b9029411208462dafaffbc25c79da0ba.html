Support USC Marshall and USC Leventhal

Consistently ranked among the nation’s premier business schools, USC Marshall School of Business and USC Leventhal School of Accounting have elevated to become global education leaders. Our schools offer an unparalleled educational experience, focused on student success and innovative research. The Marshall approach delivers real-world experience, study abroad opportunities and outstanding faculty.

Keep USC Marshall and USC Leventhal at the forefront of global business education.

The schools could not have achieved its present level of excellence without the generous support from our alumni, parents and friends who keep our schools on their list of top philanthropic priorities. We invite you to partner with USC Marshall and USC Leventhal to continue to transform the world of business education and invest in our future. The need to raise annual unrestricted funds to support and enhance the daily efforts of the schools is critical to move USC Marshall and USC Leventhal forward.

Every gift regardless of size positions our school for excellence.

The Deans rely on the annual support to the Marshall Dean’s Strategic Fund and Leventhal Dean’s Strategic Fund. This funding enables the deans to transform global business education for our students and faculty. By making a contribution of any amount to these funds, you provide critical funding to allow us to attract the best talent possible and provide students and faculty with tuition assistance, educational travel, research and more. Your annual support allows us to fulfill our mission to position our school for academic excellence. Make a gift today and help shape the future of business education and the future for our students and faculty.

Examples of how annual giving make a difference:

  • Support international student programs like GLP, LINC and PRIME
  • Fund student scholarships and fellowships
  • Recruit and retain the best faculty
  • Advance programs within the student curriculum, e.g., international case
  • Competitions, expanded career development resources and more

Support your school and invest in academic excellence today!

Alumni Participation in Giving

Your gift will help USC Marshall School of Business and USC Leventhal School of Accounting remain competitive leaders in global business education. Alumni gifts not only benefit our students and faculty, but also increase our "alumni participation rate."

What is a participation rate?

The participation rate is the percentage of alumni who provide financial contributions to the university during the year. This is not a measure of the size or amount of the gift, but rather the number of graduates who give. This percentage is calculated each and every year. In addition to providing financial support to the University, the participation rate directly affects how we are ranked against our peers. U.S. News & World Report uses the alumni participation rate as the only indicator of alumni satisfaction when it publishes its annual ranking of colleges and universities. As our ranking increases, the value of your degree increases, as well.
