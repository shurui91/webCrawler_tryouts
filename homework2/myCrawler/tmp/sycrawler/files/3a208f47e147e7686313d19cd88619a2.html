Welcome to USC Marshall School of Business Undergraduate Program Admissions

As a top-ranked four-year business school housed at an elite private research institution, a USC Marshall business degree will take you farther than you ever imagined.

Our curriculum will give you a strong grounding in business fundamentals while also providing the flexibility to pursue your personal and professional interests. Your four-year schedule of classes allows for numerous electives from across the university, and you may also choose from a wide array of minors, including entrepreneurship, accounting, cinema, sports business, and many others.

Our world-class faculty and dedicated staff are here to help you succeed. Use the menu on the left to get started and to find more information.

Don’t see what you’re looking for? Contact USC Marshall Admissions.
