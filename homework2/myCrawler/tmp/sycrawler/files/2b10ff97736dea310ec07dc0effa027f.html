Why Marshall?

Why Marshall?

  • Marshall Leads the Way Ranked Top 10 in marketing, international business, accounting, entrepreneurship and real estate
  • Business from day one 4-year program that lets you start business classes, embark on global travel, meet mentors and begin career planning in the first year
  • Family matters Global Trojan Family – nearly 400,000 alumni strong – provides unparalleled networking opportunities
  • Learn from the Best Approachable and knowledgeable professors bridge theory and practice
  • L.A. is leading ahead One of the planet’s most vibrant and dynamic metropolises serves as a learning lab for understanding diverse business environments
  • Experiential learning Hands-on courses get you on the ground, in the field and ready to go
  • Best and brightest Selective leadership and honors programs mean you’ll compete with the global elite
  • Connections that last An exceptional school attracts exceptional people; you’ll be surrounded by talented peers who become lifelong friends
  • Global crossroadsA diverse student, faculty and community culture stimulates and engages – and reflects the wider world
  • Unrivaled spirit Trojans give back, pitch in and Fight On!
     

The Best and the Brightest

The Best and the Brightest

Our undergraduates are leaders. Thinkers. Doers. Representing the best and brightest students from every corner of the United States and more than 100 countries, they come together for a powerful and engaging educational experience that equips them to take on the world.

At Marshall, students are ambitious, collegial and accomplished. Eager to expand business knowledge and to address critical challenges facing industries and societies, they turn to Marshall as an unparalleled training ground for understanding the inner workings of the global marketplace.

After four years of undergraduate study in business, Marshall students have the confidence, skill and experience to write their own tickets to professional success. They join an influential global network of more than 80,000 graduates, across 123 countries and all 50 states, whose enduring commitment to USC fosters invaluable lifelong connections.
